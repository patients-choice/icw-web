Meet The Leaders Transforming Healthcare

Our leadership team brings together decades of experience in healthcare, infrastructure, and clinical research to drive innovation and excellence in patient care.

Executive Leadership

Ron Goncalves

Co-Founder, Chief Executive Officer & President

Nationally respected healthcare executive with over two decades of leadership experience spanning healthcare delivery, hospitality, logistics, and infrastructure innovation.

Ron served as President and General Manager for Nomi Health in Florida, where he orchestrated one of the largest healthcare mobilizations in the Southeastern United States. His oversight facilitated the deployment of COVID-19 testing, vaccinations, and coordinated care to nearly 12 million Floridians, including the creation of mobile health units, municipal partnerships, and employer-based wellness clinics.

Ron has also held senior executive roles at Burger King Corporation, Norwegian Cruise Line Holdings, and Starboard Cruise Services. His reputation as a visionary builder of integrated systems, cross-functional teams, and real-time service delivery models has positioned him as a national leader in public-private health transformation.

Board Involvement:
  • • Voices For Children Foundation – Advocate for foster care justice and child wellness.
  • • CEOs Against Cancer (American Cancer Society) – Champion for early detection initiatives and national fundraising.
  • • Former Board Member, Enterprise Florida – Supported economic development and innovation statewide.
Education:
  • • B.S., Business Administration – University of Florida
  • • MBA – Florida International University (FIU)
Gabriela B. Ibarra

Co-Founder & Chief Commercial Officer

Forward-thinking executive with over 15 years of leadership experience in healthcare strategy, infrastructure innovation, and community advancement.

Prior to co-founding ICW, Gabriela was Vice President of Development at Nomi Health, where she led multi-agency contracts and statewide deployment of health access programs during the COVID-19 crisis. Her cross-industry career includes leadership in transportation, hospitality, and public affairs, providing her with a uniquely holistic approach to healthcare access.

Gabriela is recognized for her ability to unite civic, corporate, and clinical partners around shared outcomes, positioning ICW as a leader in innovative wellness delivery.

Community & Philanthropic Leadership:
  • • Chair, American Cancer Society's Evening of Hope Gala – Regional leader in fundraising for cancer research and support services.
  • • Advocate, Voices For Children Foundation – Supporting justice and advocacy for youth in the foster care system.
Education:
  • • Florida International University (FIU)
Raymond Levy

Senior Partner, Strategy & Systems Development

Senior healthcare strategist and systems integration expert with over 15 years of experience in clinical operations and infrastructure planning.

Raymond has been instrumental in scaling ICW's regional partnerships with municipalities, helping launch school-based wellness programs, deploy modular clinics, and integrate preventive care strategies for underserved communities. His operational leadership ensures regulatory compliance, cost-efficiency, and impact measurement across all divisions.

He is a champion of data-informed decision making and cross-agency collaboration, ensuring ICW's infrastructure and services deliver measurable outcomes.

Partners & Department Heads

CEO, ICW ALOYD LLC | Founder & CEO, EventStar Structures
