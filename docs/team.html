A Team United by Vision, Leadership, and Community Impact

At ICW Group, our founders are united by a shared mission: to transform healthcare access, delivery, and equity across South Florida—and beyond. Rooted in the principles of innovation, compassion, and community collaboration, ICW Group integrates clinical excellence with infrastructure and operational strategy to deliver scalable, community-driven solutions for those who need them most.

Our leadership team is composed of visionary professionals who have made significant contributions across industries including healthcare, public infrastructure, traffic and civil engineering, government, business strategy, and operations management. Together, they are building a dynamic ecosystem that merges care delivery, infrastructure, technology, and public-private partnerships into a unified platform for transformative, long-term health impact.
